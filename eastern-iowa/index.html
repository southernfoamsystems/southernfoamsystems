Comprehensive Insulation Solutions for Homes & Businesses

Finding the best insulation for your property's attic, crawlspaces, walls, or floors starts by understanding your options. iFOAM of Eastern Iowa will complete a comprehensive inspection of your current insulation and energy use to identify areas that need attention. We can then present our recommendations and ensure you have a good understanding of what will be done, the products we'll use, and how long it will take to complete.

You can turn to our Cedar Rapids insulation contractors for the highest-quality installation services. 

Our team will provide effective solutions that include:

  • Spray Foam Insulation: This is one of our most popular insulation products for its ability to expand and seal even the smallest cracks, gaps, and crevices. The result is a tight thermal barrier with better air quality and savings.
  • BATT Insulation: Made from fiberglass or wool, BATT insulation comes in pre-cut sheets and is ideal for joists, beams, and other smaller spaces.
  • Blown-In Insulation: Perfect for unfinished attics or walls, blown-in blanket or cellulose insulation can deliver an even, seamless, and long-lasting thermal barrier with minimal disturbance to your property.
  • Air Sealing: With weatherproofing, caulking, and other methods, we can identify and fix leaks that could be costing you money and comfort.
  • Insulation Removal: Is your current insulation outdated, damaged, or plain inefficient? Trust us to safely remove and dispose of the old stuff and install new, efficient insulation in its place!

Our team is equipped with the latest expertise and skills to handle any insulation project tailored to suit your building's specific needs. From start to finish, we ensure quality and satisfaction. Take advantage of our free consultation today to see what type of insulation may be right for you!
